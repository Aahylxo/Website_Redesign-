UAE E-Invoicing Rollout

The phased mandate, in plain English.

The rollout — set by Ministerial Decision 244/2025 and Cabinet Decision 106/2025 — opens with voluntary onboarding in July 2026, becomes mandatory for large taxpayers in January 2027, and reaches the rest of the economy by July 2027.

VOLUNTARY

1 July 2026

Voluntary go-live opens

Any UAE business

Onboard early without penalty. Start issuing structured invoices through your accredited ASP.

PHASE 1

1 January 2027

Large taxpayers — mandatory

Annual revenue ≥ AED 50M

ASP must be appointed by 31 July 2026. Every invoice reported through DCTCE within 14 days.

PHASE 2

1 July 2027

All other VAT-registered

Revenue < AED 50M

ASP must be appointed by 31 March 2027. ~82% of UAE businesses fall in this band.

PHASE 3

1 October 2027

Government entities (B2G)

UAE government bodies

B2G in scope. ASP must be appointed by 31 March 2027. B2C remains out of scope.

CABINET DECISION 106/2025 Penalties for non-compliance
AED 5,000/month — failure to implement
AED 100/invoice in wrong format
AED 1,000/day — system failure unreported
